Mission

To continue the healing and teaching ministry of Jesus Christ and seek to serve not only the Union – wide community but beyond by promoting healthful living, caring for the sick and sharing the good news of the Loving God.

Vision
  • - KADC becomes a center that provides quality dental care by qualify and well trained staffs.
  • - KADC employees perform to their best duties and abilities by following the example of Christ of providing physical and spiritual healing to every patient who comes for treatment without any discrimination.
  • - KADC fosters a caring Christian environment.
  • - KADC provides spiritual ministry to patients and clients and the community at large
Our Values

  • - Integrity
  • - Compassion
  • - Respect to the opinion of others
  • - Innovation
  • - Commitment in our work and call
